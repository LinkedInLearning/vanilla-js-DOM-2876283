HTML5 Cheat Sheet

Sectioning Content

article
Represents a complete, self-contained composition within a document.
aside
Content that isn't directly related to the parent section, frequently displayed as a sidebar.
nav
Contains links to other pages or sections within the current document.
section
A generic subgrouping of content within a larger document or application.

Reference the HTML5 W3C Recommendation dated 28 October 2014.
